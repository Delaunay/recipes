Brownie
=======

Ingredients
~~~~~~~~~~~

* 2.6 oz chocolate
* 1/2 cup butter
* 1/2 cup sugar
* 1/2 cup brown sugar
* 2 eggs
* 2 tsp vanilla extract
* 1/4 tsp salt
* 2/3 cup flour (= 10.5 tbsp)
* 8 tsp cocoa powder


Instructions
~~~~~~~~~~~~

#. Add everything in order
#. Mix in between
#. Bake in small muffin tin
#. 350F 10-15min
