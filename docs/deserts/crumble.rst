Apple Crumble
=============

Ingredients
~~~~~~~~~~~

* 4-8 Apples
* 1/4 cup of sugar
* 1 cup of flour
* 1/3 cup of butter
* Cinnamon
* one portion of vanilla Sugar


Instructions
~~~~~~~~~~~~

.. code-block:: python

   batter = sugar / 2 + flour + butter

   while batter is not sand like:
       mix(batter)

   apples = dice(apples)

   crumble = apples + batter

   cook(crumble, 180°C / 356°F, 45 minutes) 

Sides
~~~~~

* Vanilla Ice Cream
* Whipped Cream
* Caramel
