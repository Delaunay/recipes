Chili Oil
=========

#. 20 cloves of chopped Garlic 
#. 4 tbsp of Sichuan Peppercorn 
#. 25 whole Red Chilli 
#. 2 tbsp of Red Chilli Flakes 
#. 4 cups of  Oil (I use grapeseed oil) 
#. 2.5 tbsp of Korean Chilli Flakes 
#. 2 Star Anise 
#. 2 Tbsp of Salt
