==============

Ingredients
~~~~~~~~~~~

* 60 gr Flour
* 40 cl Milk
* Muscade
* 4 Eggs
* 150 gr Emmental
* 60 gr Butter

Instructions
~~~~~~~~~~~~

#. Pre-heat oven 180 C
#. Melt butter with flour, mix for 1h, add luke warm milk, whisk
#. Put the egg yolk with the cheese, add salt, pepper and muscade
#. Add the egg white
#. Pour the mix
#. Cook for 35 minutes
